Common checks

  • Confirm Apple Health permissions are enabled for Precision Coach.
  • Confirm Bluetooth permission is enabled before using a Bluetooth heart-rate strap.
  • Confirm Location permission is enabled before recording outdoor workouts with GPS.
  • For Apple Watch workout data, open the iPhone app after the watch workout has synced.
  • For Full Reports Unlock, use the App Store purchase restore option if you changed devices.

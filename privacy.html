Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 11, 2026

Overview

Precision Coach respects your privacy. The app uses Apple Health data only after you grant permission through iOS HealthKit.

Health and fitness data is used to calculate and display recovery, training load, heart-rate zones, sleep summaries, workout history, widgets, and reports. These insights are for fitness and training management only and are not medical advice.

Health data used by the app

With your permission, Precision Coach may read or write supported health and fitness data, including:

  • Heart rate, HRV, and resting heart rate
  • Sleep data, VO2max, steps, and active energy
  • Workout history and supported workout sessions recorded in the app
  • Location data processed on device for GPS workout recording when you start an outdoor workout

Data storage and sharing

  • Health data stays on your device.
  • Precision Coach does not send health data to a developer server.
  • Precision Coach does not use third-party analytics SDKs or crash reporting SDKs.
  • Precision Coach does not require an account or login.
  • Precision Coach does not collect your name, email address, payment information, or location for developer use.
  • Precision Coach does not sell personal data and does not use health data for third-party advertising or tracking.

Purchases

In-app purchases are handled by Apple's StoreKit and the App Store. Precision Coach reads purchase entitlement status to unlock app features, but does not collect payment card information.

Your controls

You can manage or revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in the Apple Health app or iOS Settings. You can also delete app data by deleting the app from your device.
